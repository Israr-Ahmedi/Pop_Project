About Us

ElegantPOP specializes in creating exquisite Plaster of Paris designs that transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary environments. With over 10 years of experience, our skilled craftsmen deliver precision and artistry in every project.

We take pride in our attention to detail, commitment to quality, and dedication to customer satisfaction. From conceptualization to installation, we work closely with our clients to bring their vision to life while ensuring functionality and durability.

Our team stays updated with the latest trends and techniques in POP design to offer innovative solutions that enhance the aesthetic appeal and value of your property.

Our Services

False Ceilings

Elegant and functional false ceiling designs that enhance your space's aesthetics while hiding wiring and providing better insulation.

  • Custom design patterns
  • LED lighting integration
  • Soundproofing options
  • Moisture-resistant solutions

Wall Panels

Transform plain walls into artistic statements with our custom POP wall panels that add texture and character to any room.

  • 3D wall designs
  • Textured finishes
  • Custom artwork integration
  • Decorative moldings

Mouldings & Cornices

Add elegance and sophistication with our detailed cornice work and decorative mouldings that create seamless transitions between walls and ceilings.

  • Crown mouldings
  • Decorative columns

  • Custom design elements
